Welcome to ASU Polytechnic

This app is to help you in finding particular buildings and labs that you will need in your time at the College of Technology and Innovation.

Just go to the Select Building page, choose the building you would like to locate and a map will open up with the building marked with a pitchfork. Click the pitchfork to to see an image of the building and a list of the labs located in it.
